My dog Tosca

Tosca is a 10 year old, Old English Sheepdog. She is very patient and calm making her a great family dog!

Tosca needs daily grooming and loves to go on long walks or hikes.

Breed history:

Old english sheepdogs originated in the southwestern counties of england in the 17th century to drive sheep and cattle to market.
